Apotris

Apotris is a free, open-source, multiplatform block-stacking game with highly responsive controls, deep customization, and 14 game modes. It offers both casual and competitive play, including local multiplayer, and can be played on many platforms beyond its original Game Boy Advance design.

Techmino

Techmino is a fast-paced block-stacking game focused on competitive and high-skill gameplay. It features multiple game modes, smooth and responsive controls, and advanced mechanics designed for experienced players, while still offering options for practice and customization.

Jtris

Jtris is a lightweight, classic block-stacking game for PC that focuses on simple, fast gameplay. It emphasizes efficiency and speed, offering a straightforward experience inspired by traditional Tetris mechanics without heavy customization or extra features.

Infidhells

InfidHells is a fast and challenging arcade-style block-stacking game focused on survival and high difficulty. It emphasizes intense gameplay, precise control, and quick decision-making, appealing mainly to experienced players looking for a tough, no-nonsense challenge.

Tetraesports

Tetra eSports is a modern block-stacking game focused on competitive play, fluidity, and strategy, while remaining accessible and fun for players of all skill levels. It offers a rebalanced, fixed-tuned experience that gives genre veterans a fresh competitive challenge.

Nuketris

Nuketris is a fast-paced, minimalist block-stacking game focused on speed, precision, and high difficulty. It strips the gameplay down to its core mechanics, making it especially appealing to experienced players who enjoy intense, skill-based challenges.

Worldwide Combos

Worldwide Combos is a competitive block-stacking game mode centered on building and maintaining long combo chains. It emphasizes speed, consistency, and strategic stacking, challenging players to optimize their play to achieve the highest possible combo counts on a global scale.

Notris Foes

Notris Foes is a competitive block-stacking game that focuses on strategic play against opponents. It introduces unique mechanics and enemy-based challenges that add depth and variety, encouraging players to adapt their stacking strategies to overcome different foes.
